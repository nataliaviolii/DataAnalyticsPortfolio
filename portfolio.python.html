Instacart Grocery Basket Analysis

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Company

Instacart is an American delivery company that operates a grocery delivery and pick-up service in the United States and Canada. The company offers its services via a website and mobile app. The service allows customers to order groceries from participating retailers with the shopping being done by a personal shopper. Wikipedia

Current Situation

Instacart already has very good sales, but they want to uncover more information about their sales patterns. The Instacart stakeholders are most interested in the variety of customers in their database along with their purchasing behaviors.

Key Questions

  • The sales team needs to know what the busiest days of the week and hours of the day are.
  • They also want to know whether there are particular times of the day when people spend the most money.
  • Are there certain types of products that are more popular than others?
  • What’s the distribution among users in regards to their brand loyalty?
  • Are there differences in ordering habits based on a customer’s region?
  • Are there differences in ordering habits based on a customer’s loyalty status?
  • Other key questions can be found in my Final Report - Instacart Grocery

    Objective

    The objective of this project is to analyze customer behavior and uncover patterns for growth.

    • The busiest days are Friday (6), Saturday (0) and Sunday (1). The least busy days are Tuesday (3) and Wednesday (4).

  • What’s the distribution among users in regards to their brand loyalty?


  • New Customer: Total orders are below 10

  • Loyal Customer: Total orders are above 40
    • Loyalty status is dominated by regular customers

    Most of the frequent customers come from regular to loyal status. Conversely, non-frequent customers come from customers with new status.

    Frequent customer: median from days_since_prior_order is less than 10 days

    How often do they return to Instacart?

  • Loyal customer: About 5 days
  • Regular customer: About 9 days
  • New customer: About 17 days
    • Therefore, regular and loyal customers tend to return in a short time to buy other products.

    RESULTS

    Sample results:
  • Busy hours: 09:00 AM till 04:00 PM
  • Busy days: Friday, Saturday, and Sunday
  • Instacart customers are predominately regular customers who have total orders between 10 & 40 and have an average median prior order of about 9 days.

    • RECOMMENDATIONS

  • Place some ads at the end of peak hours around 4:00pm. Research shows that customers are more likely to engage with ads when they are tired and when they do feel tired, they are more vulnerable. Also place more ads over the weekend.
  • Provide time-limited coupons/discounts
    • Provide time-limited coupons/discounts for three high-demand departments (Produce, Dairy Eggs, and Snacks), if possible, especially for those new customers. By giving them a limited discount that can be redeemed within a week, it is expected to increase their frequency of repurchasing items on Instacart from an average of 17 days to under 10 days.
